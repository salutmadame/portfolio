Meet the Teacher

Madeleine Long

Dynamic, knowledgeable, committed.

I am a K-6 teacher currently finishing my Master of Teaching and OCT certification at OISE, University of Toronto. A museum educator by trade, I value interdisciplinary inquiry, hands-on learning, and the stories behind the objects and systems that make up our lives.

I have experience teaching in museums, maker spaces, educational gardens, community theatres, and classrooms. Still, it feels better to say that all places can be learning places – we can thrive where we’re planted!

My goals as an educator are to approach each group of learners that I meet as a vibrant, changing entity, to ground my teaching in social justice, and to work collaboratively with all members of the community to build relationships, educate equitably, make art, and explore the world.
